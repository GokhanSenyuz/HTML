About Us

With over 30 years of industry leading experience, Not Just Cubes is a complete supplier of office equipment and associated technologies. We specialize in systems furniture and have a vast expertise in all office products.

Our business philosophy is a simple one: We give our clients the best service available anywhere. This means carefully coordinating a project from beginning to end.

To achieve this goal, we provide a team for each project: an account manager, a project coordinator familiar with every stage of the project, and a project manager who supervises all deliveries and installations, creates and processes the punch list and conducts final inspection.

Whether you're a firm of five or five thousand, Boston Install looks forward to the opportunity to earn your business, and to help ensure your continued success.

Whether you're installing new product or reconfiguring your existing office furniture, our experienced staff will help you to create a working environment that will help maximize your productivity.
