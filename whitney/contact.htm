The WHITNEY HOUSTON MUSIC FOUNDATION (WHMF) appreciates the fans and the public from around the world who visit the website and reminisce on the life and times of the late Whitney Houston. The foundation also hopes that Whitney Houston herself would have been proud of our efforts.
