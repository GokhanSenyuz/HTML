Booking a Ticket

Before your travels begin, you need to check if you need a travel visa and be sure your passport is valid. Every country now requires a valid passport to cross their border even if no visa is necessary.

Exotic Travel happily provides assistance in renewing passports, adding passport pages and getting visas. Our travel document specialist, will review your application as many times as necessary before sending it to the appropriate Consulate or Embassy.

Our applications are up to date with the most recent Embassy requirements, which are constantly changing. Applications are hand carried into the Consulate or Embassy as opposed to being mailed.

Rush and emergency processing is available for most visas and passports can be completed in two business days if needed. We can also help with information on getting visas upon arrival at your destination airport.


For more information about a particular passport or visa service, please contact us at tickets@exotictravel.com or 617-266-7646.
